Camps & Lessons

Riding Lessons

Our professional and friendly staff teach lessons to beginners and advanced riders, from children as young as 5 years up to and including adults. Lessons are taught by fully qualified experienced Instructors who are Garda vetted and trained in First aid and Child protection.

Prices

  • 1 hour group lesson : €25.00
  • 1 hour private lesson : €50.00
  • 1/2 hour private lesson : €30.00
  • Block of 6 group lessons: €125.00
  • Block of 10 group lessons : €200.00

Pony Camp

We run regular children Pony Camps throughout the Summer and during school holidays. Pony camps at Comeragh Equestrian are a great opportunity for kids to learn and familiarize themselves with ponies and horses through fun and hands on experience under full-time supervision.

Topics Covered

  • Grooming
  • Horse Sense
  • Feeding & Nutrition
  • Stable Management
  • Lots of fun with ponies!

Facilities

Yard

  • Purpose built wash-down bay
  • Fully heated tack room
  • Drying/rug room & tearoom
  • Large well-ventilated stables

Arena

  • 50 X 70m floodlit arena
  • Show jumps & dressage arena
  • 20 X 40m sand arena

Things to note

  • Hats available to borrow for free
  • Riders must wear suitable footwear
  • Body protector recommended
  • First time riders must register
