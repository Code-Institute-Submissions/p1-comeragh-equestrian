Livery

At Comeragh Equestrian we offer a wide range of livery services to meet the needs of you and your horse factoring in your goals, lifestyle and budget. Our livery packages range from full livery and assisted DIY to schooling livery for busy owners, coaching livery for competitors and competition livery for owners. We aim to work with all clients to create a flexible livery arrangement to suit their needs. Our yard is family run with someone onsite 24/7.

Benefits

  • Use of both outdoor arenas
  • Horses are turned out every day
  • Free horsebox parking
  • Free lessons in our AIRE approved riding school
  • Competitive rates for private lessons
