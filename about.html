About

Comeragh Equestrian is a family run Livery Yard & Riding School set in over 20 acres at the foot of the Comeragh Mountains in Co. Tipperary. Our livery clients enjoy the yards relaxed, friendly atmosphere and the peace of mind gained from leaving their horses in professional hands.
